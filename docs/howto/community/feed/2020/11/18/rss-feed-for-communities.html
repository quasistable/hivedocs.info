    ### How to get RSS feed for your community and blogs with [Ecency](https://ecency.com)? In this post, we want to share you some tricks on how to get updates or notifications to your email when your favourite author makes a post or when community you enjoy reading has something new or even when someone makes a post in certain topic or #tag. And automate many possible tasks with few simple clicks and without any programming skills.
    ![RSS-feed-communities-blog](https://images.ecency.com/DQmdih1Cz3hcmeJCxnuqMePDVPN9aSN59ux54MW1GobdJbw/rss.png)
    RSS feeds are great way to get notified, if you like reading and learning about new skills, you have ability to setup automated notification to your email. Tools like [Blogtrottr](https://blogtrottr.com), [Feedrabbit](https://feedrabbit.com), [Zapier](https://zapier.com), [IFTTT](https://ifttt.com) helps you to automate RSS feed for free and easily. You can also automate post sharing to your social media, for example if your friend makes a post you might want to get notified and auto share their post to your channels on [Twitter](https://twitter.com) or [Instagram](https://instagram.com) or [Facebook](https://facebook.com). #### With Ecency, you can find RSS feed for every author/user, every tag with filter and every community. Open your profile on [Ecency](https://ecency.com) and find `RSS feed` left bottom of screen. - RSS feed for your blog, example for `@good-karma` looks like this: `https://ecency.com/@good-karma/rss.xml` - RSS feed for your community, example for posts on `Ecency` community looks like this: `https://ecency.com/created/hive-125125/rss.xml` `https://ecency.com/trending/hive-125125/rss.xml` `https://ecency.com/hot/hive-125125/rss.xml` - RSS feed for topics you love, example of Newly created `DIY` (do it yourself) topics with #diy tag looks like this: `https://ecency.com/created/diy/rss.xml` `https://ecency.com/trending/diy/rss.xml` `https://ecency.com/hot/diy/rss.xml` With examples above, you can setup automated distribution of **your content to Social media sites** (RSS to Instagram, RSS to Twitter, RSS to Facebook, RSS to Slack, RSS to Telegram, RSS to Wordpress, RSS to LinkedIn, **RSS to Discord**, **Receive email notifications**, **create newsletters** and many more possibilities. Try it out and let us know what best use cases you tried for your blog or community with RSS feeds.
