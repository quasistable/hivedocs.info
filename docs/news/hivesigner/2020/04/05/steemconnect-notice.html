    SteemConnect notice

    Apr 5, 2020 • good-karma

    In this post we would like to update everyone about discontinuation of [SteemConnect](https://steemconnect.com). It has been incredible few years serving all users and dapps on Steem, but now that we have migrated to [Hive](https://hive.io) as [Hivesigner](https://hivesigner.com). It is time to focus our time and effort making [Hivesigner](https://hivesigner.com) the best authentication service, it can be on [Hive](https://hive.io). # Sunset date We are setting a date to **stop [SteemConnect](https://steemconnect.com) services on 1st June, 2020**. We kindly request all Dapp developers to plan and inform their users about changes. # Migration options We will actively be improving [Hivesigner](https://hivesigner.com) and encourage developers to build on [Hive](https://hive.io). But if for some reason you want to stay on [Steem](https://steem.io), please migrate to some other fork of [SteemConnect](https://steemconnect.com) - for example [SteemLogin](https://steemlogin.com). ***We are not in any way connected with any forks, as always do your own research, audit and security of new forks.*** # Hivesigner development For all new developers, visitors, [SteemConnect](https://steemconnect.com) will be redirecting everyone to [Hivesigner](https://hivesigner.com) homepage and help them get started with development on [Hive](https://hive.io). We will be adding FAQ and more detailed documentation on How to integrate [Hivesigner](https://hivesigner.com) to your website, How to authenticate with [Hivesigner extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/hivesigner/ophihnhnfgcmhpbcennhppicomdeabip), How to sign transactions securely, etc. We have published roadmap and proposal to support continuous development of the project. Please read more [about it here and support our effort](https://esteem.app/hive/@good-karma/hivesigner-ongoing-development-and-improvement) bringing secure and social authentication to the masses. --- * [Vote for Hivesigner Proposal](https://hivesigner.com/sign/update-proposal-votes?proposal_ids=%5B88%5D&approve=true) * Github: [ledgerconnect/hivesigner](https://github.com/ledgerconnect/hivesigner) * Discord support: https://discord.gg/pNJn7wh * Email: `hello@hivesigner.com`
